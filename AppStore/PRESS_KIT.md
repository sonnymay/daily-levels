# Daily Levels — Press Kit & Apple Featuring nomination

## Elevator pitch (one line)
**Daily Levels turns focus into a daily climb — every 5 minutes you stay focused levels up a
little RPG hero, and it all resets at midnight for a fresh run.**

## What it is
A calm, one-screen native iOS focus timer with a light-RPG skin. Focus 5 minutes = 1 level; your
hero grinds while you work (and *keeps* grinding while your phone is locked), rests when you switch
apps, and climbs a 10-class ladder from Novice to **Mythic**. No tabs, no settings, no noise — just
one screen and a reason to come back tomorrow.

## Why it's featurable (the editorial hooks)
- **A genuinely novel hook in a crowded category** — "focus = leveling up a hero" is RPG motivation
  done calmly, not a points-and-streaks Skinner box. Differentiated from Forest/Finch/Flora.
- **Privacy by design** — App Privacy label is **"Data Not Collected."** No accounts, no analytics,
  no ads, no tracking, no third-party SDKs. Everything stays on device (SwiftData + StoreKit 2 only).
  Apple loves to feature privacy-respecting apps.
- **Native & modern** — 100% SwiftUI, iOS 17 (`@Observable`, SwiftData), zero third-party
  dependencies. Showcases the platform.
- **Calm / digital-wellbeing angle** — deliberately anti-dark-pattern: no anxiety countdowns, an
  unstarted day never "breaks" your streak, gentle haptics, Reduce-Motion support, full VoiceOver,
  Dynamic Type. Fits "Apps We Love / Focus / Wind Down / Get Things Done" stories.
- **Localized** into 6 languages (EN, ES, PT-BR, DE, FR, JA).
- **Indie, one-time unlock** — free to start, a single **$6.99** "Pro" unlock (no subscription) to
  evolve the hero to Mythic. A wholesome indie story.
- **Seasonal fit** — students are the core audience; **back-to-school (Aug–Sep)** and New Year are
  prime featuring windows.
